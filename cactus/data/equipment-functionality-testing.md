# Equipment Functionality Testing

## Overview
Equipment Functionality Testing is a systematic process designed to verify that a piece of equipment or a system performs its intended operations and functions correctly, as per its design specifications and requirements. This involves thoroughly checking all components and subsystems to ensure they work together seamlessly, meet performance benchmarks, and operate reliably and safely. The primary goal is to identify any defects, errors, or performance bottlenecks early on, before the equipment is deployed for its intended use. This proactive approach helps guarantee that the equipment is fit for purpose, prevents potential operational failures, and minimizes costly downtime. It's a critical step in quality assurance for any hardware or system.
