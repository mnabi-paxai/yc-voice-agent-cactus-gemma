# Anonymity

## Overview
Anonymity refers to a state where a person's identity is unknown or concealed. This means that they cannot be easily identified or linked to their actions, statements, or presence. It's like being able to participate or observe without anyone knowing exactly who you are. This can be intentional, to protect privacy or enable free expression, or unintentional, simply because identifying information is not available.
