# Session Recording Test

## Overview
A Session Recording Test is a crucial check performed to ensure that all equipment intended to capture data during a research session (such as video, audio, or screen recordings) is functioning correctly and saving the information as expected. It's a proactive measure to prevent the loss of valuable research data due to technical malfunctions, confirming that the recording mechanism is active and storing the captured content.

## Key Ideas
- The primary objective was to confirm that the session recording functionality was operational.
- This test specifically aimed to verify that video data was being successfully saved.
