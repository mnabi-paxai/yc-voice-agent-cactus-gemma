# Content Attribution

## Overview
Content attribution is the practice of acknowledging and giving credit to the original creator, source, or owner of information, ideas, or work. It is a fundamental principle for maintaining intellectual honesty, respecting intellectual property rights, and ensuring transparency about the origin of content. Proper attribution prevents plagiarism, allows others to verify information, and enables them to explore the original source for further details or context. It is essential in academic, professional, and digital environments to build trust and uphold ethical standards.
